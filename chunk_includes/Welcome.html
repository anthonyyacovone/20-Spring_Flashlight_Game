Welcome!

Thank you for participating in this study!


In this study, you will be reading a series of sentences "in the dark" and occasionally answering comprehension questions about them. To do this, you will use your mouse as a "flashlight" to illuminate the words on your screen. Prior to seeing the sentence, you will have to click a tiny red arrow (like the one below). Then a sentence will appear. Watch the example below!
After reading it fully, you would again hit 'Enter' and then read another sentence or answer a comprehension question.

Before starting...

Make sure that your browser is MAXIMIZED, your screen zoom is set to 100% (or 'Actual Size'), and your screen brightness is turned all the way up!


Some of the sentences are tricky, so make sure to read them fully. This study typically takes an hour to complete.


Comprehension Check:

 1. Since I will be "reading in the dark," how will I see the actual sentences?



2. What do I have to click before seeing a sentence appear?



3. What do I press when I have finished fully reading the sentence?



4. Is your window in full-screen mode, your zoom set to 100%, and your screen brightness all the way up?
